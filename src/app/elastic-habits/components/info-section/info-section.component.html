Getting Started in 3 Simple Steps

  1. Create Your Habit

    Click the "+" button to add a new habit. Give it a clear name that describes the action you want to perform regularly.

  2. Set Your Three Levels

    For each habit, define your Mini, Plus, and Elite versions - from easy to challenging. For example, for "Exercise":

    Mini

    5 minutes of any movement

    Plus

    20-minute workout session

    Elite

    45-minute intense workout

  3. Track Daily Progress

    Each day, click on the level you completed. You can choose any of the three options based on your energy, time, and motivation that day.

Daily Usage Tips

  • Low energy day? Just hit your Mini level to maintain your streak.
  • Feeling motivated? Go for Elite and challenge yourself.
  • Track patterns in the weekly and monthly views to understand your consistency.
  • Schedule specific days for certain habits using the calendar feature.
  • Check your stats to see your progress and improvement over time.

Why ElasticHabits Works

ElasticHabits succeeds where traditional habit trackers fail because it adapts to real life. By offering three difficulty levels, you can maintain consistency even during busy or difficult days, while still challenging yourself when conditions are favorable.
